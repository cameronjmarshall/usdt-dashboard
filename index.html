Dashboard notes

This dashboard is designed as a browser-only crypto morning dashboard. Key points: • Core rates, supply and exchange prices are pulled live where possible. • Manual exchange volume rows and operational notes can be edited at the top of the script. • If one exchange fails or rate-limits, the page should still show the rest of the data. • Default refresh is 60 seconds to reduce throttling.
Note: Crypto news and daily commentary have been removed for now. Manual volume and operational notes remain editable in the script.
